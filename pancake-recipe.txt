Ingredients

- 1 cup of flour
- 1 cup of milk
- 1 egg

Directions:

1. Combine all the ingredients in a large bowl.
2. Heat up a frying pan, ensuring it is sufficiently greased.
3. Add a spoonful of the mixture to the pan, and cook until one of the sides starts to firm up a tad.
4. Flip the cake.
5. When the cake is done, slide that bad boy off and add whatever topping you like.
6. Eat and be Merri.
